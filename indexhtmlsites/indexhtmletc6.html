      DEBUGGING ESSENTIALS , - to open debugging or developer tools in the browser , open the browser and go to "Developer options " or press ctrl + shift + I .

      Once the developer tools are open in the browser , notice the TWO BUTTONS in the TOP LEFT corner of the Dev Tools.

      One is an Arrow , the other two squares, Click on the ARROW in the top left for a selector, select an element in a web page and see details , as well as see temporary edits on the fly , and details of lines of code in the dev options in your browser .

      Click on the square , to see different image ratio sizes. Drag the scrollbar horizontally to adjust for page size display.
